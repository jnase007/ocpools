Transparency

Our Smart Pool Process

24/7 remote monitoring meets on-demand expert service. Your pool is watched around the clock — we visit only when it actually needs us.

Always Watching

How Your Pool Is Monitored 24/7

When you sign up, we install a smart WiFi pool monitor directly in your pool — included in your contract (~$300 value). From that moment on, your pool is never unwatched.

pH Levels

Continuous pH monitoring ensures your water stays balanced and comfortable — no more guessing with test strips.

Chlorine & ORP

ORP (oxidation-reduction potential) tracks real sanitizer effectiveness — far more accurate than a basic chlorine test.

Temperature

Water temperature affects chemistry, algae growth, and comfort. The monitor tracks it 24/7 so nothing sneaks up on you.

Water Clarity

TDS and turbidity readings flag cloudiness or buildup before you can even see it — catching problems before they start.

Real-Time Data for You and Us

You see exactly what we see — through the app, anytime. Real data, real-time. No guesswork, no "trust me, it looks fine."

Automatic Alerts When Levels Drift

The moment any reading drifts out of range, TruePools gets notified instantly. We respond to data, not a calendar.

No Unnecessary Weekly Visits

We come only when your pool actually needs us — triggered by real data, not a recurring schedule. That means less disruption and smarter service.

Total Transparency

Every reading, every alert, every trend — visible to you in real-time. This is your pool. You deserve to see what's happening with it.

On-Demand Service

When We Do Visit, Here's What Happens

When the monitor flags an issue — or it's time for a scheduled physical service — our technician follows these 8 steps. No shortcuts, no mystery.

1
Arrival

Arrival & Visual Inspection

Our technician arrives at your scheduled time, checks the pool area for any visible issues — debris, algae, waterline buildup, or equipment concerns — and takes a "before" photo.

5 min
10 min
2

Skim, Brush & Vacuum

We skim the entire surface, brush the walls, steps, and tile line, then vacuum the floor. Every corner is addressed — not just the visible areas.

3

Empty Baskets & Clean Filter

Skimmer baskets and pump strainer baskets are emptied. Filter pressure is checked and cleaned or backwashed as needed to maintain optimal flow.

5 min
10 min
4

Water Testing & Chemical Balance

We test pH, chlorine, alkalinity, calcium hardness, and stabilizer levels using professional-grade kits. Chemicals are adjusted precisely — never guessed.

5

Equipment Inspection

Pump, motor, heater, salt cell, automation system, and lights are all visually inspected and tested. We catch worn seals, unusual noises, and pressure changes early.

5 min
5 min
6

Water Level & Deck Cleanup

Water level is topped off if needed. The pool deck around the work area is rinsed and left clean. We never leave a mess behind.

7

Final Check & "After" Photo

A final walkthrough ensures nothing was missed. We take an "after" photo of the pool looking its best — crystal clear, swim-ready, spotless.

2 min
Instant
8

Digital Report Sent

You receive a detailed digital report with before/after photos, chemical readings, equipment notes, and any recommendations — all within minutes of our visit.
