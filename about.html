Meet the Founder

It Started With a Kid in a Pool

I grew up in the water. Every house we lived in had a pool, and every time we moved, my parents upgraded to a bigger and better one. My best childhood memories are in the pool — summer days that never seemed to end, cannonballs, races, the sound of splashing and laughter.

When I was finally able to buy my own home, the first thing I did was design the pool I'd always dreamed of. Now my three kids have the same experience I had — and watching them make those memories is everything.

That's why I started OC Pools. I know what a great pool means to a family. It's not just water and chemicals — it's where your kids grow up, where you unwind after a long week, where you host the people you love. A clean, healthy, beautiful pool changes the way you live at home.

My goal is simple: give every family in Orange County the same experience I want for mine. A pool that's always perfect, always safe, and always ready.
